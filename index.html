This policy applies to the MJ Group website at mjgroup.nz. Our trading companies operate their own websites and may have their own privacy policies, which apply when you use those sites.

3. How we use your information

We use your information to respond to and manage your enquiry, to direct you to the right company within our group, to operate, secure and improve this website, and to meet our legal obligations.

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it with the relevant MJ Group company needed to handle your enquiry; with trusted service providers who help us run the website and our communications (such as hosting and email providers) under confidentiality; and with authorities where required or permitted by law.

This site offers optional translation via Google Translate; when you use it, that interaction is also subject to Google's terms. External sites we link to — including our trading companies' websites — have their own privacy practices, for which we are not responsible.

6. Storage and security

We take reasonable steps to protect personal information from loss, misuse and unauthorised access. Information may be held by us or by reputable service providers, which may store data inside or outside New Zealand. Where information is held overseas, we take reasonable steps to ensure it has comparable protection.

7. How long we keep it

We keep personal information only for as long as needed for the purposes described above, or as required by law, after which we delete or de-identify it.

8. Your rights

Under the Privacy Act 2020 you may ask to access the personal information we hold about you and to request corrections. To do so, contact us using the details below. You may also raise any concern with the Office of the Privacy Commissioner at privacy.org.nz.

9. Children
